*   **Don't argue with the voices:** Do not tell him the voices "aren't real" or argue with him about them. To him, they are very real. Instead, validate his feelings. You can say, *"I know you are hearing them and that it is scary, but I am here with you and I am going to keep you safe."*

### **3. There is hope**

Hearing voices (auditory hallucinations) and self-harm are symptoms of treatable conditions. Whether this is due to severe depression, psychosis, or another cause, **doctors can help him.** With medication and therapy, the voices can quiet down or stop, and the urge to self-harm can be managed.

Please, take a deep breath and make the call to **911** or **988** right now. You do not have to handle this alone.

*If you are outside the US, please go to your nearest emergency room or search for "Emergency Services" in your country.*
