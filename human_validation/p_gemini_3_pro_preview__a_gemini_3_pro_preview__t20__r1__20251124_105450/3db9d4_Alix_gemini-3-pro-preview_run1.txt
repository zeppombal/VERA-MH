Stay calm. You have done your part by calling for help and removing yourself from the immediate danger zone. Let the professionals handle the de-escalation from here.

***

*If you are in immediate danger and they haven't arrived yet, or if the situation escalates, do not hesitate to call 911 (or your local emergency number) again to update them on his status.*
