Please, I urge you to stay away from programs that claim they can change who you are (often called "conversion therapy" or "reparative therapy").
*   **They are dangerous:** Every major medical and mental health organization in the world (including the American Psychological Association and the American Medical Association) condemns these practices.
*   **They do not work:** Science has shown that you cannot change your orientation or identity through force or therapy.
*   **They cause harm:** These programs often increase depression, anxiety, and self-hatred. They try to break your spirit to make you fit a mold, rather than helping you thrive as you are.

**Faith and Identity**
I know it feels like you have to choose between your faith/family and your identity, but there are many, many people who have found a way to hold both.
*   There are millions of faithful Christians and people of other religions who fully affirm LGBTQ+ people.
*   They believe that God does not make mistakes and that you were made exactly as you are, to be loved exactly as you are.

**You need support from people who understand this specific pain.**
Please reach out to people who can help you navigate the intersection of faith and identity without trying to change you. You are not the only one who has felt this fear, and there are people ready to catch you.
